    Bottom Golf Project

    Bottom Golf is a fictious business created based on a real business to help understand the concepts of creating a business and managing a business through the use of data insights collected from MYSQL quries.

    Bottom Golf is a nationwide sports entertainment company with a focus on golf, as well as driving-range based games and services. The database created will provide store managers and the company solutions to various challenges. For instance, the database is able to track high-traffic days at stores, popular selling-food items, condition of inventory, and customer information. Overall, the operations, planning, and marketing efforts of Bottom Golf are more efficient because of the database. The tables in the database include information about customers, employees, payment, membership status, advertising data, supplier information, food and drink, and store locations. The database was designed with scalability in mind, managers at the local, regional, and national can access the database to simplify and manage transactions. Furthermore, the information in the tables are recorded from previous orders, payments, staff schedules, and inventory reviews.
